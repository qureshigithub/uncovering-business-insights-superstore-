# uncovering-business-insights-superstore-
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on Retail Sales Data
Monthly Sales Trend: Sales peak significantly in November and December.
Regional Efficiency: The West Region is the most profitable, yielding the highest average profit of $33.85 per order.
Profit Driver: The Consumer Segment is the primary engine of the business, generating nearly 47% of total profits.
Product Leaders:Phones and Chairs are the top-performing sub-categories in terms of revenue.
 Data shows that discounts exceeding 50% result in immediate losses
